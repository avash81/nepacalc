📐 Understanding the Nepal Gold Measurement System (1 Tola = 100 Lal)

In the Nepali gold market, the Tola (तोला) is the foundational baseline unit of mass. However, for smaller pieces of jewelry like rings, nose pins (Phuli), and earrings, jewelers break weights down into Aana and Lal (लाल).

The structural mathematical relationship governing the official Nepal gold unit scale follows these exact ratios:

  • 1 Tola = 100 Lal
  • 1 Tola = 16 Aana
  • 1 Tola = 11.6638 Grams
  • 1 Aana = 6.25 Lal
  • 1 Lal = 0.116638 Grams (commonly rounded to 0.1166g)

If a jeweler tells you an asset weighs 12 Aanas, that equates exactly to 75 Lal, which represents precisely 0.75 Tola of pure gold.

🧮 Mathematical Conversion Constants & Formulas

How to Convert Lal to Gram

Because 1 Tola equals 100 Lal and weighs exactly 11.6638 grams, a single Lal is exceptionally light.

Weight in Grams = Total Lal × 0.116638

Example: If you want to find out how many grams are in 15 Lal: 15 × 0.116638 = 1.7495 grams.

How to Convert Gram to Lal

If you have a digital kitchen scale or a laboratory balance measuring an item in grams, translate it back to traditional units using this division formula:

Weight in Lal = Weight in Grams ÷ 0.116638

Frequently Asked Questions

How many grams are in 1 Tola of gold in Nepal?

One Tola of gold equals 11.6638 grams according to the official Nepal gold measurement system. One Tola is also equal to 100 Lal or 16 Aana.

How many Lal are in 1 Gram?

1 Gram equals approximately 8.5735 Lal using the official Nepal gold measurement standard. You can convert Gram to Lal instantly using the Nepal Gold Unit Converter, which uses the official conversion factor based on 1 Tola = 11.6638 grams = 100 Lal.

How many Lal (Laal) are in 1 Tola?

One Tola contains exactly 100 Lal (Laal). This is the standard conversion used by jewellery shops and the Federation of Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers' Association (FENEGOSIDA).

How many Aana are in 1 Tola?

One Tola equals 16 Aana. Each Aana is equal to 6.25 Lal or approximately 0.729 grams.

How do I convert Gram to Lal?

Divide the weight in grams by 0.116638. Example: 1 gram = 8.5735 Lal, 5 grams = 42.87 Lal, 10 grams = 85.74 Lal. The Nepal Gold Unit Converter performs this calculation automatically.

How do I convert Lal to Gram?

Multiply the total Lal by 0.116638. Example: 15 Lal = 1.7496 grams, 20 Lal = 2.3328 grams, 25 Lal = 2.9159 grams, 40 Lal = 4.6655 grams, 50 Lal = 5.8319 grams.

How many grams is 15 Lal?

15 Lal = 1.7496 grams, which is 0.15 Tola.

What is the difference between Hallmark and Tejabi gold?

Hallmark gold is certified for purity and generally refers to 24K (99.9%) or other certified purity levels. Tejabi gold is traditionally 22K (91.6%) and is commonly used for jewellery because it is more durable than pure 24K gold.

Who sets gold rates in Nepal?

Daily gold prices in Nepal are published by the Federation of Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers' Association (FENEGOSIDA). The Nepal Gold Unit Converter uses the official Nepal gold measurement system for accurate weight conversions, while current market prices should always be verified using the latest FENEGOSIDA rate.
