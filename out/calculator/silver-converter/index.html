Quick Answer: Silver Measurement Standards

In Nepal, 1 Tola of silver equals exactly 11.6638 grams (100 Lal, 16 Aana, or 64 Ratti) as standardized by FENEGOSIDA and the Nepal Bureau of Standards & Metrology (NBSM). Internationally, silver is traded in Troy Ounces, where 1 Troy Ounce equals 31.1035 grams or approximately 2.6667 Tolas.

Nepal Traditional Silver Measurement System (Chandi Weight)

In Nepal, silver (popularly known as Chandi) is measured using the traditional South Asian bullion system recognized by FENEGOSIDA. Understanding these units ensures accurate jewellery pricing and investment trading. You can check all current market-rate tools at our Live Market Rates Hub.

1 Tola

= 11.6638 Grams

Standard unit for silver coins, bars, and heavy ornaments in Nepal.

1 Lal

= 0.1166 Grams

Used for small silver work. Exactly 100 Lal make 1 Tola.

1 Aana

= 0.7290 Grams

Traditional sub-unit. Exactly 16 Aanas make 1 Tola.

1 Ratti

= 0.1822 Grams

Gemstone & fine silver unit. Exactly 64 Ratti make 1 Tola.

International Silver Measurement System

Global silver commodity markets (such as LBMA and COMEX) trade silver in metric and troy units:

  • Gram (g): Metric unit commonly used for retail silver jewellery worldwide.
  • Troy Ounce (t oz): The global standard benchmark unit for precious metals. 1 Troy Ounce = 31.1035g (equivalent to approx 2.6667 Tolas).
  • Kilogram (kg): Industrial and institutional silver bar unit. 1 Kilogram = 1,000g = 85.7353 Tolas.
  • Avoirdupois Ounce (oz): Standard commercial ounce = 28.3495g (used for general goods, distinct from Troy Ounce).

Silver Conversion Formulas

Tola to Grams

Grams = Tolas × 11.6638

Example: 5 Tola × 11.6638 = 58.319 Grams

Grams to Tola

Tolas = Grams ÷ 11.6638

Example: 50 Grams ÷ 11.6638 = 4.286 Tola

Troy Ounce to Grams

Grams = Troy Oz × 31.1035

Example: 2 t oz × 31.1035 = 62.207 Grams

Silver Purity Guide (999 Fine vs 925 Sterling)

Not all silver items contain 100% pure silver. Alloy metals are added for strength and durability:

999 Fine Silver

99.9% Pure Silver

Highest commercial purity. Soft and malleable, used almost exclusively for investment bullion bars and official minted silver coins.

10 Tola 999 = 116.64g pure silver

925 Sterling Silver

92.5% Pure Silver

The international jewellery standard. Mixed with 7.5% copper for structural strength in rings, chains, bracelets, and fine ornaments.

10 Tola 925 = 107.89g pure silver

800 Jewellery Silver

80.0% Pure Silver

Commonly used in Nepal for traditional heavy utensils, puja items, anklets (Payal), and decorative silver items requiring high rigidity.

10 Tola 800 = 93.31g pure silver

Calculating Silver Market Value & Budget

Silver value in Nepal is calculated by taking the weight in Tolas and multiplying by today's FENEGOSIDA silver rate per Tola:

Total Value (NPR) = (Weight in Grams ÷ 11.6638) × Silver Rate Per Tola × Purity %

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How many grams are in 1 Tola of silver in Nepal?

One Tola of silver equals exactly 11.6638 grams according to the official Nepal measurement standards established by NBSM and FENEGOSIDA.

How many Lal are in 1 Tola of silver?

There are exactly 100 Lal in 1 Tola of silver. Therefore, 1 Lal equals approximately 0.1166 grams.

How many Aana are in 1 Tola of silver?

There are 16 Aana in 1 Tola. Each Aana equals approximately 0.7290 grams of silver.

What is 925 Sterling Silver?

925 Sterling Silver contains 92.5% pure silver and 7.5% copper or other alloy metals to give the jewellery rigidity and strength.

What is 999 Fine Silver?

999 Fine Silver is 99.9% pure silver, which is the highest purity grade used for bullion investment bars and official coins.

How do I convert Troy Ounces to Tola?

One Troy Ounce equals 31.1035 grams. To convert to Tola, divide 31.1035 by 11.6638, which yields approximately 2.6667 Tolas per Troy Ounce.

How many Tolas are in 1 Kilogram of silver?

One Kilogram (1,000 grams) of silver equals approximately 85.7353 Tolas.

How is silver price calculated in Nepal?

Silver price in Nepal is calculated per Tola based on daily published FENEGOSIDA rates, adjusted for purity grade and jeweller making charges.
