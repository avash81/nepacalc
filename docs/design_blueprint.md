# 🔬 NepCalc: The 10 Perspective Design Blueprint (Google + University)

This document is the **Full Site Perspective** benchmark. It combines **Google's Clinical Precision** with **Top University Information Density** across 10 core UI/UX states.

---

## 🏛️ 1. The Home Page Authority (Google Standard)
The focal point of the platform. Designed for immediate trust.
- **Visual Style**: Massive "Sheet-on-Surface" background contrast. Pure white console on a soft-gray field.
- **Primary UX**: Google Search-style central calculator. 
- **Typography**: Google Sans / Inter (Weighted for authority).

---

## 📱 2. Mobile-First Lab Workspace
Optimized for one-handed clinical performance and total readability.
- **Visual Style**: Unified 5-column grid for category navigation.
- **Primary UX**: Floating "Nepal Command Bar" with fixed Z-index and high-contrast badging for "Secured" status.

---

## 📊 3. Internal Financial Intelligence (University Standard)
High-contrast output cards for SIP, EMI, and Savings.
- **Visual Style**: Clinical results on white elevation cards (#FFFFFF).
- **Primary UX**: Real-time ROI visualizations and interactive loan schedule tables.

---

## 🔦 4. Universal Command Search (Ctrl+K)
Instant tool navigation inspired by Google Workspace.
- **Visual Style**: Deep modal with high-contrast results and category filters.
- **Primary UX**: Full keyboard accessibility for finding 75+ tools in under 2 seconds.

---

## 📁 5. The Resource Matrix (Directory)
The authoritative map of every scientific and financial tool.
- **Visual Style**: Horizontal category chips with friction-scrolling. 
- **Primary UX**: 3D-effect cards with "Hot" and "New" pulse indicators.

---

## 🌑 6. Midnight Precision (Dark Mode)
Engineering-grade high-focus environment.
- **Visual Style**: True deep midnight backgrounds (NASA-specification) with luminous blue accents.
- **Primary UX**: Total readability in low-light technical sessions.

---

## 🩺 7. Health Laboratory Perspective
Clinical status indicators for medical and physical tools (BMI, BMR).
- **Visual Style**: High-fidelity gauge charts (Material 3).
- **Primary UX**: Immediate, color-coded health-rating feedback (Green/Amber/Red).

---

## 🏗️ 8. Engineering Schematic Perspective
Specialized for Civil and Physics tools (Concrete Mix, Physics).
- **Visual Style**: Diagrammatic output grids and technical notation fonts.
- **Primary UX**: Tabular data output optimized for construction site use.

---

## 📖 9. Academic Guide UX (The Reading Room)
The "University Portal" experience for long-form guides and blogs.
- **Visual Style**: Minimalist, high-legibility sans-serif reading margins.
- **Primary UX**: Integrated table of contents and internal citation links.

---

## 🏥 10. The Authoritative Anchor (The Footer)
The final trust marker and platform directory.
- **Visual Style**: High-density 5-column grouping (University portal style). 
- **Primary UX**: Validated ISO markers and "NEPAL EDITION" authentication badges.
